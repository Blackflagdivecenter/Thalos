## Description

Thalos is a professional scuba dive log, planner, and safety companion — built for every diver from student to instructor.

**DIVE LOGBOOK**
Log training and recreational dives with full version history. Track marine life sightings, attach photos, and record buddy signatures. Organize dives by trip. Import profiles from Garmin dive computers via FIT files.

**510+ DIVE SITES**
Browse a curated library of dive sites across 13 world regions with GPS coordinates, depth ratings, conditions, and access notes. Save custom sites with emergency action plans (EAPs).

**9 DIVE CALCULATORS**
- Buhlmann ZHL-16C Decompression Planner (GF Lo/Hi, CCR, multi-gas)
- Gas Planner (6 modes including trimix, CNS tracking)
- Gas Blending (empty tank & top-off modes)
- NAUI Recreational Dive Tables
- SAC Rate & Gas Consumption
- Turn Pressure (Rule of Thirds, Rock Bottom, Gas Matching)
- Weight Calculator (Archimedes physics model)
- Unit Converter (depth, pressure, temperature, volume, weight)
- Altitude Diving (TOD, NDL adjustments, flying after diving)

**EMERGENCY MODE**
One-tap access to emergency contacts, site-specific EAPs, and 8 dive emergency procedures. Works fully offline — no internet required.

**INSTRUCTOR MODULE**
Manage students, run courses from 26 built-in templates, track skill sign-offs per environment, handle paperwork (liability release, medical questionnaire, training dive logs) with digital signatures.

**COMMUNITY**
Discover dive classes, trips, and dive centers posted by the community. Collaborate with dive buddies — share profiles, social handles, and dive media in real time.

100% free. No ads. No subscriptions. No tracking. Your dive data stays on your device.
